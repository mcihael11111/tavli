## Full Description (4000 chars)

Tavli (Τάβλι) brings the soul of Greek backgammon to your phone. Play on stunning, Manopoulos-inspired wooden boards against Μιχαήλ (Mikhail) — an AI opponent with a warm Greek personality who evolves from a friendly uncle to a fierce legendary grandfather across 5 difficulty levels.

🎲 AUTHENTIC BACKGAMMON
• Complete backgammon rules — perfectly implemented
• 5 difficulty levels from beginner to expert
• Smart move highlighting and undo
• Doubling cube with strategic AI decisions
• Full tutorial guided by Mikhail himself

🎨 PREMIUM BOARD AESTHETICS
• 3 photorealistic board sets inspired by Manopoulos craftsmanship
• Μαόνι: warm mahogany & olive wood
• Σμαραγδί: rich mahogany & Aegean teal
• Νυχτερινό: dark walnut, navy & copper
• 3 checker sets: Classic, Ruby, Olive
• Mix and match boards, checkers, and dice

🤖 MEET ΜΙΧΑΗΛ (MIKHAIL)
• An AI opponent with genuine character — not just an algorithm
• Speaks in a charming mix of Greek and English
• Each difficulty level reveals a different side of his personality
• Easy: warm, encouraging uncle who tells kafeneio stories
• Medium: competitive regular who celebrates his doubles
• Hard: intense veteran with no patience for slow play
• Παππούς: the legendary grandfather — 60 years of mastery
• Teaching mode: learn strategy with Greek proverbs and expert tips

🌐 PLAY ONLINE
• Quick match with rating-based matchmaking
• Invite friends via username, QR code, or link
• Elo rating system with Greek-themed ranks
• From Αρχάριος (Beginner) to Θρύλος (Legend)

🎵 KAFENEIO ATMOSPHERE
• Mediterranean acoustic soundtrack
• Authentic wood and dice sound effects
• Warm kafeneio ambiance

No ads. No pay-to-win. Just beautiful, authentic tavli.
