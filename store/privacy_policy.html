Tavli — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 19, 2026

Tavli ("we", "our", "the app") is a mobile backgammon game developed for Android and iOS. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: If you create an account, we collect your email address, display name, and profile avatar. You may also sign in via Google or Apple, in which case we receive your name and email from those services.

Gameplay Data: We collect game statistics (wins, losses, rating, match history) to provide leaderboards and track your progress.

Device Information: We collect anonymous device information (device model, OS version, app version) for crash reporting and performance monitoring via Firebase Crashlytics.

Analytics: We use Firebase Analytics to understand how players use the app. This data is aggregated and anonymous.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not access your contacts, camera, or microphone (QR scanning uses camera only during active scanning).
  • We do not sell or share personal data with third parties for advertising.
  • We do not display ads.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the game service
  • To match you with online opponents
  • To track your game statistics and rating
  • To send push notifications about game invites and your turn (with your permission)
  • To improve the app through crash reporting and analytics

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase services (Google Cloud Platform), which comply with SOC 1, SOC 2, and ISO 27001 standards. Game data is encrypted in transit using TLS.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Tavli is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it.

6. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

  • Access: You may request a copy of your personal data.
  • Deletion: You may request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Portability: You may request your data in a portable format.
  • Opt-out: You may disable analytics in Settings.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@tavli.app.

7. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, analytics, crash reporting
  • Google Sign-In: Optional authentication
  • Apple Sign-In: Optional authentication (iOS)

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.

9. Contact Us

For questions about this privacy policy: privacy@tavli.app
